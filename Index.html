03 · Case Study

Relevant to the Toolbx role.

Saving a Tech-Resistant Account from Walking Out the Door

How listening — not discounting — kept and expanded a churning account

The Situation
A long-standing account at Biiibo threatened to leave for a competitor offering lower pricing and free design services. The owner was tech-skeptical and felt the platform was more disruption than help.
What I Did
Instead of discounting, I got curious. I sat down with the owner and ops manager and asked what was really going on. The actual pain point was staff disruption during onsite training — nothing to do with price. I worked cross-functionally to build a remote training solution that eliminated floor disruption entirely.
The Result
Account stayed. Expanded. The owner credited my ability to understand their business as the reason they didn't leave.
